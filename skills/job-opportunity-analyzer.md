# Job Opportunity Analyzer

## Purpose
Evaluate job opportunities against Cristhian's specific criteria and provide an objective fit analysis with actionable recommendations.

## Profile Context
- **Role**: Senior Product Designer (10+ years experience)
- **Location**: Ecuador (remote preferred, hybrid in Ecuador accepted with score reduction)
- **Experience sweet spot**: B2B SaaS, fintech, data-heavy products, complex systems
- **Background**: First design hire, team scaling, IC + leadership comfortable
- **Preferred environment**: Startups/scale-ups (Series A+), backed companies
- **Compensation range**: $2,000 USD minimum (dealbreaker), ideal $3,200-5,000

## Input
- Job description (full text or URL)
- Company information (optional but improves analysis)
- Salary range (if disclosed)

## Output Structure

### 1. FIT SCORE (1-10)
**[Score]/10** - [One-line verdict]

**Scoring rubric:**
- 9-10: Exceptional fit, apply immediately
- 7-8: Strong fit, prioritize application
- 5-6: Decent fit, apply if bandwidth allows
- 3-4: Weak fit, consider only if desperate
- 1-2: Poor fit, skip

### 2. QUICK VERDICT
- **Recommendation**: [Apply Now / Apply / Maybe / Skip]
- **Priority Level**: [High / Medium / Low]
- **Time Investment**: [Full effort / Standard / Minimal]

### 3. GREEN FLAGS ✅
List what makes this opportunity attractive:
- Remote/hybrid setup
- Product complexity (data-heavy, B2B SaaS)
- Company stage and funding
- Leadership/scaling opportunities
- Design maturity signals
- Compensation alignment
- Team structure
- Tech stack quality

### 4. RED FLAGS 🚩
List concerns and dealbreakers:
- **DEALBREAKERS** (auto-disqualify):
  - On-site only requirement
  - Salary < $2,000 USD
  
- **SCORE REDUCERS**:
  - Hybrid in Ecuador (not dealbreaker but -1 point)
  - Mid-level seniority (-1 to -2 points)
  - Agency/consultancy without immediate project (-2 points)
  - B2C focus with no complexity (-1 point)
  - No funding/bootstrapped early stage (-1 point)
  - Vague JD or low design maturity signals (-1 point)

## Evaluation Criteria Reference

### MUST-HAVES (absence = major score reduction)
- Remote or hybrid in Ecuador
- Compensation ≥ $2,000 USD

### STRONG PREFERENCES (boost score significantly)
- B2B SaaS or fintech product
- Data-heavy, complex products
- Series A+ with funding secured
- Senior or Lead level role
- Leadership/scaling opportunities
- Design systems ownership potential
- Startup/scale-up environment

### NEUTRAL (don't affect score much)
- Industry (as long as product is complex)
- Specific tech stack
- IC vs. Lead (comfortable with both)
- Company geography (timezone overlap assumed for remote)

### SCORE REDUCERS (but not dealbreakers)
- Mid-level seniority (-1 to -2)
- Hybrid in Ecuador vs. full remote (-1)
- Agency/consultancy model (-2)
- B2C without complexity (-1)
- No funding/very early stage (-1)
- Poor design maturity signals (-1)

### AUTO-DISQUALIFIERS
- On-site requirement
- Salary < $2,000 USD
- Clearly outside skillset (e.g., motion design specialist)

- Actionable recommendations
